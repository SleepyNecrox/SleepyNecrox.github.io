Project Description

The Black Death has risen. Cleanse those plagued in shadows, in this alchemical survival horror.

Play as the Plague Doctor hired to cleanse the residents of Vale, Alchemist Academy. In search of the Hidden Formula to defeat The Black Death. Explore and solve puzzles for Hidden Formulas, to Infuse your cane with alchemy concoctions.

Inspired by old Playstation graphics similar to Resident Evil 2, and Silent Hill 2, and similar games like Alan Wake, and Puppet Combo.
