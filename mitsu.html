Project Description

Manage a Cat Cafe during the day and explore the town at night to adopt and rescue cats... Though be careful, cats are expensive...

Made in 15 days for the Cause Jam 35: Make Games, Kids Win. Hosted by Games For Love, a nonprofit organization that provides video games, consoles, and therapy to children in hospitals and facing other challenges, using play as a form of distraction therapy to ease suffering, save lives, and create sustainable futures.

Game dedicated to My BEAUTIFUL girlfriend and her cats (Mitsu), and children in hospitals who could use a furry friend.
