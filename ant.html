Project Description

Ant Colony Defense is a 2.5D Tower Defense game where you play as a Queen Ant controlling your subjects to defend your ant hill. Place down ant units to defend your base from incoming enemy colony attacks. The game takes place at some human‚Äôs poorly maintained garden. Their inability to watch over it allowed ant colonies to form, which eventually got to the extent that they began fighting for territory within it

Attacking ants will use pathfinding to find the best way to reach the ant hill and attack whoever is in their way. Defending ants will cycle through states depending on how close the invaders are to them and will react accordingly.

Inspired by games such as Clash Royale, Plants vs Zombies, and Battle Cats!
